Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Apr 25, 2025

Introduction

Quick UC is a browser extension designed to provide auto-login functionality for frequently used websites by students of Pontificia Universidad Cat√≥lica de Chile (PUC). Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Quick UC does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data provided by the user is stored locally on their device.

Data Usage

  • Login Credentials: The extension stores your login credentials (e.g., username and password) locally on your device to enable auto-login functionality. These credentials are never transmitted to any external server or third party.
  • User Preferences: Preferences such as selected services and auto-login settings are stored locally to personalize your experience.

Data Sharing

Quick UC does not share any data with third parties. All data remains on your device and is used solely for the purpose of providing the extension's functionality.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • `tabs`: To open new tabs for login redirection and interact with the currently active tab.
  • `storage`: To store user preferences and login credentials locally.
  • Host Permissions:
    • https://sso.uc.cl/cas/login*: To enable auto-login for services like Portal UC, Canvas, and Tarjeta UC.
    • https://cas-v2.mat.uc.cl/jwt/login?app=labmat-v2: To enable auto-login for LabMat services.

These permissions are strictly used to provide the intended functionality of the extension.

Security

We take the following measures to ensure your data is secure:

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of the extension and documented in the release notes.
